# BACA Product

**Status:** Active
**Type:** AI-native project management platform
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-19

## What We're Building

An AI-native project management platform where AI agents autonomously execute tasks within a governed, auditable workflow. Humans set direction and maintain governance; agents do the execution.

## Target Customer

Technical founders at high-growth startups (2–20 engineers) who want to scale output without proportional headcount growth.

## MVP (5 features)

1. Autonomous Agent Task Execution
2. Human-in-the-Loop Governance (approval gates)
3. Agentic Hierarchy & Delegation
4. Full Issue Lifecycle Management
5. Run Observability Dashboard

## Thesis

The next generation of high-output teams will be hybrid — humans govern, AI agents execute. First mover with the right trust primitives wins the agentic PM category.
